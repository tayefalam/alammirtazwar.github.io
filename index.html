Key Project — Case Study

Car Damage Detection through Optimization of VGG16 CNN

📌 Problem

Existing CNN models rely heavily on large datasets and fail to detect subtle damages (scratches, dents) effectively when trained on limited or non-diverse data.

⚙️ Approach

Leveraged transfer learning with VGG16, built preprocessing pipeline using OpenCV, applied targeted augmentation and fine-tuning.

✅ Solution

Improved detection accuracy for small, reflective damages by ≈14% mAP over baseline. Robust pipeline reduces overfitting.

📈 Impact

More stable validation under constrained datasets; real-world usability for automated damage assessment systems.
